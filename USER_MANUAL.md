## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

**As a new volunteer:**
1. Visit your organisation's VolunteerOS link (e.g. `https://yourorg.volunteeros.app`).
2. Click **Sign up** and enter your email address and a password (minimum 8 characters).
3. Check your email for a confirmation link and click it to activate your account.
4. Complete your volunteer profile (see [Setting Up Your Profile](#setting-up-your-profile)).

**As a coordinator or admin:**
Your account is created by a Super Admin. You will receive an invitation email — click the link and set your password.

---

### Logging In

1. Go to your organisation's VolunteerOS URL.
2. Enter your email and password, then click **Log in**.
3. If your organisation uses Single Sign-On (SSO), click **Log in with [Provider]** instead.
4. If you forget your password, click **Forgot your password?** and follow the email instructions.

> **Locked out?** After 5 failed attempts your account locks for 30 minutes. Contact your coordinator to unlock it sooner.

---

### Navigating the Dashboard

After login you land on your personal dashboard. The top navigation bar contains:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Home** | Your dashboard — upcoming shifts, pending tasks, recent notifications |
| **Opportunities** | Browse open volunteer opportunities |
| **Schedule** | Your shift calendar |
| **Messages** | Direct and group conversations |
| **Profile** | Your volunteer profile |
| **Notifications** (bell icon) | Unread alerts |

Coordinators and admins see additional menu items: **Volunteers**, **Programs**, **Reports**, and **Settings**.

---

## For Volunteers

### Setting Up Your Profile

A complete profile helps coordinators match you to the right shifts.

1. Click your avatar (top right) → **My Profile** → **Edit Profile**.
2. Fill in the following sections:

| Section | What to include |
|---------|----------------|
| Personal Info | Full name, preferred name, pronouns, phone number, date of birth |
| Photo | Upload a clear headshot (JPG/PNG, max 5 MB) |
| Skills | Type skills and select from the autocomplete list |
| Interests | Choose categories that match your passions |
| Availability | Select the days and times you are generally available each week |
| Blackout Dates | Add any dates you are unavailable (holidays, events) |
| Max Hours | Set your weekly or monthly maximum volunteering hours |
| Emergency Contact | Add at least one emergency contact name and phone number |
| Credentials | Upload any required documents (ID, certifications); set expiry dates |

3. Click **Save Profile**.

> **Minors:** If your date of birth indicates you are under 18, some content is restricted until a coordinator approves safeguarding documentation.

---

### Finding & Applying for Opportunities

1. Click **Opportunities** in the top nav.
2. Use the filters to narrow results by **Category**, **Location**, **Date**, or **Commitment Level**.
3. Switch to **Map View** to browse opportunities near you.
4. Click an opportunity card to view full details.
5. Click **Apply Now**.
   - **New volunteers** complete a short application form.
   - **Returning volunteers** can apply with one click.
6. Answer any custom application questions and submit.

You will receive an email confirmation. Your application status will show as **Applied** in **My Applications**.

**Application statuses:**

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Applied | Received; awaiting coordinator review |
| Shortlisted | You are being considered |
| Approved | You have been accepted |
| Waitlisted | All spots are full; you will be notified if one opens |
| Declined | You were not selected for this opportunity |

---

### Completing Onboarding

After approval, you may need to complete onboarding steps before your first shift.

1. Go to **My Onboarding** (visible in your dashboard when steps are pending).
2. Complete each step in order:
   - **Watch video** — play the embedded video to completion.
   - **Read document** — open and read the document; click **Mark as Read**.
   - **Quiz** — answer all questions; a passing score is required to proceed.
   - **Upload document** — attach the required file and submit.
   - **Sign form** — tick the checkbox and confirm your name to sign electronically.
   - **Attend induction** — mark attendance at a scheduled session.
3. Your progress bar updates after each completed step.
4. Once all steps are complete, your profile status moves to **Active**.

> If you do not complete onboarding within the required period you will receive reminder emails. Contact your coordinator if you need help.

---

### Viewing and Signing Up for Shifts

1. Click **Schedule** in the top nav.
2. Use **Month**, **Week**, or **Day** view, or switch to **List View**.
3. Shifts matching your skills, location, and availability are highlighted as **Suggested for you**.
4. Click a shift to see full details (location, time, coordinator, remaining spots).
5. Click **Sign Up** to confirm your place. You will receive an email/SMS confirmation.

**Cancelling a shift:**
- Click the shift on your calendar → **Cancel My Spot**.
- Cancellations within the cut-off window (set by your coordinator) are flagged as late cancellations.

**Requesting a swap:**
- Click the shift → **Request Swap** → search for an available volunteer to swap with → **Send Request**.
- The swap is confirmed once the other volunteer accepts and the coordinator approves.

---

### Checking In to a Shift

**QR code check-in (recommended):**
1. Open VolunteerOS on your phone at the shift venue.
2. Go to **Schedule** → tap the current shift → **Check In**.
3. The camera opens; scan the QR code displayed by the coordinator.

**Manual check-in:**
Your coordinator can mark you as checked in from their attendance list.

**Geofenced check-in (if enabled):**
When you arrive within the venue radius, your phone prompts you to check in automatically (requires location permission).

---

### Logging Hours

**Shifts:** Hours are logged automatically when you check out. If there is a discrepancy, click **Dispute Hours** on the shift record, add a short note explaining the issue, and submit. Your coordinator receives an in-app notification and will review the discrepancy.

**Ad-hoc activities:**
1. Go to **My Hours** → **Log Hours**.
2. Select the program, enter the date, number of hours, and a short description.
3. Click **Submit**. The hours appear as **Pending** until a coordinator approves them.

**Hour log statuses:**

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Pending | Awaiting coordinator review |
| Approved | Counted towards your cumulative total |
| Rejected | Not accepted; you will receive a notification with the reason |
| Disputed | You have flagged an auto-logged entry for coordinator review |

---

### Viewing Your Hours & Milestones

- **My Hours** shows a breakdown by program and date range, with a cumulative total at the top.
- Cumulative totals are shown on your personal dashboard and update in real time as hours are approved.
- When you reach a milestone (e.g. 10, 50, 100 hours) you receive an in-app notification and email, and any associated badge is awarded automatically.
- Export your hours as CSV from **My Hours** → **Export**.

**Milestone thresholds** (configured per organisation):

| Milestone | Default Threshold |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Getting Started | 10 hours |
| Active Volunteer | 50 hours |
| Dedicated Volunteer | 100 hours |
| Champion | 500 hours |

Your coordinator can set custom thresholds and attach a badge to each milestone.

---

### Messaging & Notifications

**Direct messages:**
1. Click **Messages** → **New Conversation**.
2. Search for the coordinator or volunteer by name.
3. Type your message (supports rich text, attachments) and press **Send**.

**Notification preferences:**
Go to **Profile** → **Notification Settings** to choose which alerts you receive and on which channels (in-app, email, SMS).

---

### Badges & Recognition

- Earned badges appear on your profile under **Achievements**.
- Click a badge to see how it was earned.
- Click **Share** on any badge to generate a LinkedIn or social share link.
- You can request a PDF reference letter from **Profile** → **Request Reference**.

---

### Deactivating Your Account

Go to **Profile** → **Account Settings** → **Deactivate Account**. Your profile is hidden from coordinators but your data is retained. To permanently delete your data (GDPR right to erasure), select **Request Data Erasure** and confirm.

## For Coordinators

### Managing Volunteer Profiles

Navigate to **Volunteers** to access the full roster.

- **Search/Filter:** Use the search bar or filter by status, program, skill, or custom field.
- **Edit a profile:** Click a volunteer's name → **Edit Profile**.
- **Archive a leaver:** Open the profile → **Actions** → **Archive**.
- **Merge duplicates:** Select two profiles → **Merge Profiles** → confirm which record to keep as primary.
- **Bulk import:** Click **Import Volunteers** → download the template → fill in data → upload the CSV/Excel file → map columns to fields → confirm import.
- **Export roster:** Click **Export** → choose CSV or Excel.

---

### Creating Opportunity Listings

1. Go to **Opportunities** → **New Opportunity**.
2. Fill in title, description, required skills, location, dates, spots available, and commitment level.
3. Set status to **Draft** to save without publishing, or **Published** to make it live.
4. Copy the **Public URL** to share externally, or use the **Embed Widget** code to add to your website.

---

### Managing the Application Pipeline

1. Go to **Opportunities** → select an opportunity → **Applications**.
2. The Kanban board shows columns: **Applied**, **Shortlisted**, **Approved**, **Declined**, **Waitlisted**.
3. Drag application cards between columns, or use the dropdown on each card.
4. Select multiple cards for **Bulk Approve** or **Bulk Decline** with a templated message.
5. Waitlisted applicants are automatically promoted when a spot opens.

---

### Building Onboarding Checklists

1. Go to **Programs** → select a program → **Onboarding**.
2. Click **New Checklist** and give it a name.
3. Add steps: click **Add Step** and choose step type (video, document, quiz, upload, sign, induction).
4. Drag steps to reorder them.
5. Set a **Stall Reminder** (e.g. send reminder after 3 days of no progress).
6. Assign the checklist to a role or directly to volunteers.

**Quiz builder:**
- Choose step type **Quiz** → **Build Quiz**.
- Add questions (multiple choice or short text) and mark correct answers.
- Set a minimum passing score.

---

### Creating and Managing Shifts

1. Go to **Schedule** → **New Shift**.
2. Choose **Single Shift**, **Recurring Shift**, or **Multi-Day Event**.
3. Fill in title, program, location, start/end time, roles needed, capacity, and notes.
4. For **Recurring Shifts**, configure the recurrence pattern (daily/weekly/custom) and end date.
5. Enable the **Waitlist** toggle if you want overflow capacity managed automatically.
6. Click **Save**.

**Clone a shift:** Open an existing shift → **Clone** → adjust dates → save.

**Export schedule:** Go to **Schedule** → **Export** → choose PDF (print-ready) or iCal feed.

---

### Scheduling Volunteers

**Manual assignment:**
1. Open a shift → **Manage Volunteers** → **Add Volunteer**.
2. Search by name, skill, or availability.
3. Click **Assign**.

**Auto-fill suggestions:**
1. Open a shift with unfilled slots → **Auto-Fill**.
2. Review the ranked list of suggested volunteers (sorted by fit score: skills + availability + proximity).
3. Click **Assign** next to each suggestion.

**Drag-and-drop timeline:**
1. Go to **Schedule** → **Timeline View**.
2. Drag volunteer names from the sidebar onto shift blocks.
3. Conflicts (double-booking, outside availability) are highlighted in red.

---

### Tracking Attendance

1. Open a shift → **Attendance**.
2. Click **Generate QR Code** to display the check-in code for volunteers to scan.
3. Toggle volunteers manually between Present / Absent / Late.
4. After the shift ends, the system automatically flags any missing attendance records and sends follow-up messages.

---

### Approving Hours

1. Go to **Hours** → **Pending Approval**.
2. Review each submission (volunteer, date, hours, description, and source: auto / self-logged / bulk import).
3. Click **Approve**, **Reject**, or **Edit & Approve**.
   - **Edit & Approve** lets you correct the hours or description before approving.
   - When rejecting, enter a short reason — the volunteer is notified automatically.
4. For bulk actions, select multiple rows → **Bulk Approve**.
5. To skip manual approval for a program, go to **Program Settings** → enable **Auto-Approve Hours**.

**Disputed hours:**
Disputed entries (auto-logged hours flagged by a volunteer) appear in a separate **Disputed** tab. Review the volunteer's note, then approve the corrected total or keep the original and reject the dispute.

**Bulk CSV upload:**
1. Go to **Hours** → **Bulk Import**.
2. Download the CSV template.
3. Fill in columns: `volunteer_email`, `program_slug`, `shift_id` (optional), `date` (YYYY-MM-DD), `hours`, `description`.
4. Upload the file. A background job processes each row and creates `HourLog` records with source `bulk`.
5. Results (successes and errors) are emailed to you when processing completes.

---

### Sending Messages & Broadcasts

**Direct message:**
Click **Messages** → **New Conversation** → search for a volunteer or group → compose and send.

**Broadcast:**
1. Go to **Messages** → **New Broadcast**.
2. Build your audience segment using filters (role, program, shift, custom field, etc.).
3. The **Live Preview** shows the estimated recipient count.
4. Choose delivery channels: in-app, email, SMS, WhatsApp (if enabled).
5. Compose your message using the rich-text editor.
6. Click **Send Now** or **Schedule**.

**Email templates:**
Go to **Settings** → **Email Templates** to edit per-event templates, preview them, and add personalisation tokens.

---

### Generating Reports

1. Go to **Reports**.
2. Select a standard report from the list (e.g. Hours by Program, Shift Fill Rate).
3. Set the date range and any filters.
4. Click **Generate**.
5. Export as CSV, Excel, or PDF.

**Custom report builder:**
1. Click **New Custom Report**.
2. Drag dimensions and metrics into the builder.
3. Add filters.
4. Click **Run** to preview results.
5. Click **Save** to name and store the report.
6. Optionally set up a **Scheduled Delivery** (daily/weekly/monthly) to receive the report by email.

---

### Issuing Badges & References

**Award a badge:**
1. Go to a volunteer's profile → **Badges** → **Award Badge**.
2. Choose from the badge library or create a custom badge.
3. Optionally add a personal note.

**Generate a reference:**
1. Go to a volunteer's profile → **References** → **Generate Reference**.
2. The PDF pre-populates with verified stats (hours, programs, tenure).
3. Download or send directly to the volunteer.

## For Organisation Admins

### Organisation Settings

Go to **Settings** → **Organisation**.

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Branding | Upload logo, set primary colour, configure email sender name/address |
| Timezone & Locale | Set the organisation's default timezone and date/distance format |
| Notification Defaults | Set default notification channels for all users (overridable per user) |
| Terms & Privacy | Upload volunteer-facing ToS and privacy policy documents |
| Data Retention | Configure how long inactive records are kept before archival or deletion |

---

### Managing Users & Roles

**Create a coordinator account:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Users** → **Invite User**.
2. Enter email, assign a role, and optionally restrict to specific programs.
3. Click **Send Invitation**.

**Custom roles:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Roles** → **New Role**.
2. Clone a base role (Coordinator, Read-Only Staff) and toggle individual permissions on/off.
3. Save and assign to users.

**Remove a user:**
Go to **Settings** → **Users** → find the user → **Deactivate** or **Delete**.

---

### Configuring Integrations

Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** to connect third-party services.

| Integration | Setup Steps |
|-------------|-------------|
| Google Calendar | Click **Connect** → authenticate with Google → select which calendars to sync |
| Outlook Calendar | Click **Connect** → sign in with Microsoft account |
| Slack | Paste your Slack incoming webhook URL → select the channel |
| Salesforce | Enter your Salesforce domain and API credentials → map fields |
| Stripe | Enter your Stripe publishable and secret keys |
| Twilio SMS | Enter your Twilio Account SID, Auth Token, and sending number |
| Zapier | Copy the VolunteerOS API key and paste into Zapier when creating a Zap |

For the **REST API**: go to **Settings** → **API** → **Generate API Key**. The interactive API documentation is available at `/api/docs`.

---

### Data Retention & GDPR

**Process a right-to-erasure request:**
1. Go to **Volunteers** → find the volunteer → **Actions** → **Erase Personal Data**.
2. Review the summary of data to be anonymised.
3. Confirm. The job runs in the background; PII is replaced with anonymised tokens while aggregate statistics are preserved.

**Export a volunteer's data (GDPR Subject Access Request):**
Go to the volunteer's profile → **Actions** → **Export Data** → download ZIP.

## Account & Security

### Changing Your Password

Go to **Profile** → **Account Settings** → **Change Password**. Enter your current password and your new password twice, then click **Save**.

### Two-Factor Authentication

Go to **Profile** → **Account Settings** → **Two-Factor Authentication** → **Enable**. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy) and enter the 6-digit code to confirm.

### SSO Login

If your organisation has configured SSO (SAML 2.0 or OpenID Connect), click **Log in with [Your Provider]** on the login page. You will be redirected to your identity provider and returned automatically after authentication.

---

## Getting Help

- **In-app help:** Click the **?** icon in the bottom-right corner to access contextual help articles.
- **Email support:** Contact your organisation's VolunteerOS administrator, or email support at the address listed in the app footer.
- **Status page:** Real-time system status is available at your organisation's status page URL (provided by your admin).

> If you believe you have found a security issue, please report it responsibly via the contact details in the app footer rather than publicly disclosing it.
